Hi! I'm Zahra Al-Sultan

With over 3 years of experience working with authors, I help writers take their writing to the next level.

While studying at MSU, I have honed my knowledge in storytelling, grammar, research, proofreading, and more. These are all skills that I use now when working with writers. I have worked with writers for 3 years both academically and professionally to help refine their writing and make their message clearer. I am dedicated to being a reader's advocate and making sure writers are publishing the absolute best work they have to offer.
